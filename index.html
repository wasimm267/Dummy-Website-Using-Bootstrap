Welcome

Talk about Kolkata food and you'll instantly picture sugar-dripping Roshogullas, chenna-based sweets and mouth-watering fish preparations that hound the popular notion of Bengali cuisine

Foods To Enjoye Here

Maccher Jhol

Machher jhol or machha jhola is a traditional spicy fish curry in Bengali and Odia cuisines in the eastern part of the Indian subcontinent

Kolkata Biryani

Kolkata biryani is a huge favourite not only in Kolkata, but also outside. However, it wasn't introduced as an alternative to meat as is widely believed

Kosha Manghso

Fresh goat meat cooked with potatoes in a rich ginger garlic gravy along with some tomatoes and curd in mustard oil is a recipe to keep

Luchi

Luchi is similar to the puris while Aloo Dom is the Bengali rendition of Dum Aloo. Very popular among tourists and locals, these dishes carry the essence of Bengal in them
